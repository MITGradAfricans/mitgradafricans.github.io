About Us

The MIT African Graduate Association (MIT AGA) is a vibrant community dedicated to supporting African graduate students at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. We foster academic excellence, professional growth, and cultural exchange while building lasting connections within the MIT community and beyond.

  • Mentorship and academic support
  • Professional development workshops
  • Cultural events and celebrations
  • Networking opportunities
  • Advocacy for African student interests
  • Partnerships with other organizations
